Umthetho Wobumfihlo

Ubumfihlo bakho bubalulekile kithi. Le app ayiqoqi, igcine, noma ifake noma iyiphi idatha yomsebenzisi siqu noma imininingwane eyimfihlo ye-workshop. Wonke amarekhodi akho okugqoka amathuluzi agcinwa ngokuphephile kudivayisi yangakini (noma asekelwe ngokuvumelanayo nge-akhawunti yakho ye-iCloud).

Le app ayidingi ukubhalisa, akukho okubhaliselwe, futhi ithembisa ukuthi izohlala iyithuluzi lokukhiqiza elihlanzekile.

Uma unemibuzo, sicela uxhumane nosizo.
